About Me

Hi! I am an undergraduate at the University of Connecticut, pursuing a Digital Media & Design major with a minor in Studio Arts. Originally from Northern VA, about a 20 minute drive from DC, I grew up exploring the beautiful trails along the Potomac River. Now, as more of a city girl, I love discovering new food spots in Boston.

I have a strong foundation in the Adobe Creative Cloud, including Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Adobe Illustrator. My past experience covers public relations, digital marketing, and graphic design through various organizations at my university, as well as a summer internship this past year. I’m passionate about combining creativity with technology, incorporating diversity and crafting a meaningful message through my work, and I’m always eager to learn from those with more experience and collaborate with a dynamic team. I’m excited to explore new job/internship opportunities that will help me further develop my design and marketing skills.
